About Us

About

Fonics, An ISO 9001-2008 Certified Company is based in Bangalore, India. Since its incorporation in the year 2011 has been mainly concentrated on the animal feed additives. With regular R&D in the areas of Aqua Culture, Poultry, Dairy, Piggery and Agriculture had developed quality products for its customers and clients. Fonics is using the state-of-the-art technology in producing Probiotics, Prebiotics and Enzymes. The people behind the company are expertise over years in the field. Fonics main motive is to design the quality products at affordable prices for both Local and International markets. Fonics had adopted a policy to produce pure, safe high effective probiotics, Prebiotics and enzymes for Poultry, Piggery, Cattle, sheep and Aqua Farms. Every effort, scientific and technical will be made to comply with the requirements of the market. Thus Fonics is committed to implement and execute quality assurance and control policies in accordance with international requirements. We believe our strategic planning will provide us wider access globally. Our main objective is to emerge as a professionally managed, quality-focused company and attain a high pedestal in producing Probiotics, Prebiotics and Enzymes at affordable price through innovative and eco friendly techniques.
